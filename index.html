Recommended Study Order

1
Foundation: Security Fundamentals
Start with Cybersecurity Fundamentals to establish core concepts, frameworks (NIST, ISO 27001), and the MITRE ATT&CK framework.
2
Core Security: Incident Response & Threats
Learn incident response methodologies, threat monitoring with XDR, and common attack scenarios specific to the role.
3
Infrastructure: M365, Endpoints & Network
Cover Microsoft 365 administration, endpoint protection with Intune/Defender, and Cisco Meraki network management.
4
Operations: Backup, Telephony & Support
Understand disaster recovery, business continuity, telephony systems (8x8), and IT support processes.
5
Compliance: IAM, DLP & Awareness
Review identity management, data loss prevention, compliance requirements (PIPEDA, FIPPA), and security awareness training.
6
Final Prep: Behavioral & 30-60-90 Plan
Practice behavioral questions using STAR method, complete scenario exercises, and finalize your 30-60-90 day plan.
